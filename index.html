About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Roorkee. I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the National University of Singapore (NUS). I completed my Ph.D. as a PMRF Fellow at IIT Bhubaneswar and worked at C-DOT, Govt. of India.

My research includes IoT systems, network protocols, and edge computing. I focus on designing efficient protocols for large-scale IoT, data collection for digital twins in the Metaverse, and resource allocation in IoT-Metaverse systems.
