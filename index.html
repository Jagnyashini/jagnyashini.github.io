About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Roorkee. I worked as a Postdoctoral researcher at National University of Singapore (NUS). I completed my Ph.D. as a Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) at Indian Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar. I have six years of work experience in the networking and telecommunication domain at Center For Development of Telematics (CDOT), Govt. of India. My research interests include IoT systems, network protocols, and edge computing. My work focuses on designing and developing efficient protocols for large-scale IoT systems, data collection strategies for digital twin synchronization in the Metaverse, and innovative resource allocation approaches for IoT-enabled Metaverse systems.
